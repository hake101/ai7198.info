Good Music Which I Enjoy

I mainly listen to Rock music including System of a Down and blink-182 and the Soundtracks from Games such as all three Splatoon games, Super Mario Galaxy, and Portal 2. It's all good stuff. I highly enjoy The Highway Song from System and Go from blink-182. Nintendo and Valve make absolutely amazing music for their games. Nintendo's music you can hear it easily but with Valve, they sparingly play the music so it's just the ambiance you listen to (check out the bridge portion from Half-Life 2).

My Music Listening Setup

A high-end audiophile setup is the dream but I have very heavy monetary limitations (I don't even have a job yet) so I just have 70 gigabytes worth of .flac files and on my PC I use Winamp to listen to that music. It's a great piece of software that I pair with last.fm for Discord RPC and tracking. Because I don't always have access to PC because I don't carry my laptop around, I use both Apple Music from the free trials you can get from anywhere and an iPod Video (iPod Classic 5.5th gen) with Rockbox installed. I replaced the battery which made it last longer and not heat up and freeze and still waiting for its original hard drive to die to upgrade to and iFlash Quad.
